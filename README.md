# JustExecute

**Turn your startup idea into an actionable plan in seconds.**

JustExecute is an AI-powered startup validation tool that gives you the same depth of market research that consultants charge $5,000–$15,000 for — instantly and for free.

## What It Does

🔍 **Live Web Search** — Scans the web for real-time market data, trends, and news about your niche.

💬 **Reddit & Forum Mining** — Digs through Reddit, Indie Hackers, Hacker News, and niche forums to find what real users say about similar ideas.

🏢 **Competitor Deep-Dive** — Identifies competitors, analyzes pricing, features, and market positioning.

📊 **Demand Validation** — Estimates market size, finds early demand signals, and validates willingness to pay.

⚠️ **Risk Assessment** — Detects the "Death Spiral" patterns that kill 90% of startups before you invest.

🚀 **90-Day Action Plan** — Generates a tailored go-to-market strategy with milestones and tactics.
